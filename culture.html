Our Cultural Heritage

3000 BCE

Indus Valley Crafts

1500 BCE

Vedic Period Arts

Intricate ritual objects and textile weaving.

500 BCE

Rock-cut architecture, temple sculptures, and the refinement of painting traditions.

1200 BC

Medieval Craftsmanship

Flourishing of miniature painting, textile arts, and regional folk art traditions.

1700 CE

Handloom Revolution

Rise of regional weaving centers producing world-renowned textiles and fabrics.

Today

Modern Heritage

Preserving ancient techniques while adapting to contemporary design and global markets.
